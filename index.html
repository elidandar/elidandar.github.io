Research

My research spans deep learning applications in computer vision, cybersecurity, and materials science, with a focus on automation and reliability.

Research Interests:
• Computer Vision: Segmentation (Single-shot & Interactive), Object Detection, and Region of Interest (ROI) detection.
• Cybersecurity: AI-agent-driven network traffic generation, LLM fine-tuning, and anomaly detection.
• Bioinformatics: Hierarchical Graph Neural Networks (HGNN) for gene regulatory networks.
